Amazing world design

Skyrim is a vast and expansive open world with many towns, villages, dungeons, and more to visit. Tons of npc's in populated areas to talk to. And, if you're lucky, you may even come across one in the wild. And if you're even luckier (from a certain point of view) they won't be dumb enough to attack you after witnessing you single handedly kill a dragon and devour it's soul and flesh leaving only bones behind by simply staring blankly at it's corpse.

But, more than likely they'll attack you anyway and you'll likely be low health after your amazing fight with the dragon™. Though never fear, simply inhale the 300 wheels of cheese you conveniently keep in your back pocket that you "accuired" from the nearby village and you'll be ready to face whatever had the audacity to walk too close to you.

WOW! Do you see that mountain over there? That's right, you can climb it. And so can your horse, better than you can, even.

With Skyrim's extreme realism you can climb any mountain, hill, house, rock, boat, or anything else your heart desires. Just don't slip! Or you may end up plummeting to your Doom™. Amazing! Just like real life.
You can even go higher than the talles mountain if you'd like. Simply visit the nearest giant camp and wave hello. You'll be flung to the edge of space posthaste. And don't worry, if you survive being launched into space, you won't be able to survive the return trip.

Great Community

Despite Todd's attempts at monitizing mods, the modding community has made tens of thousands of mods. Ranging from small tweaks, graphics improvements, animation changes, content additions like new items or abilities, and even complete game overhauls. With so many mods to choose from you'll always find new ways to play the game.

A team of developers has even created a completely free total conversion mod for Skyrim called "Enderal: Forgotten Stories". The team behind this game not only crafted a brand new story but also overhauled the skill systems and gameplay mechanics present in Skyrim. Additionally they restructured terrain and built all new detailed and diverse landscapes to explore.

Install enough mods and you'll eventually barely be able to recognize the game. You can even install community made bug fix mods to repair the broken mess of a game that Todd and his team left at your door step.
